3  Teaching Assistant

Leila Hojat will serve as a teaching assistant for this course, for which I am extremely grateful. Leila has taken the course in the past, and will be happy to help you with questions you have about R, about propensity scores, and about other course materials when I cannot.

Leila Hojat is a former PQHS 500 student who completed the course in Spring 2022 as part of the Masters in Biostatistics Program. She is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Medicine/ Division of Infectious Diseases at CWRU and a physician at University Hospitals. Her work focuses on antimicrobial stewardship in the hospital setting, and some of her research interests include antimicrobial resistance, rapid diagnostics, and health equity.
